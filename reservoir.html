Reservoir Control System

About

Tools: STM32 Microcontroller, Embedded C, Motors


The Reservoir Control System project seamlessly integrates embedded systems, custom PCB design, servo motors, DC motors, and RGBs for efficient water management. The embedded system ensures real-time monitoring and intelligent decision-making, while servo motors and DC motors optimize water flow and automate processes. RGBs provide visual indicators for user-friendly feedback. This innovative solution prioritizes resource optimization, operational efficiency, and adaptability, setting a new standard in intelligent water management.

Key Features

1. Embedded Systems:
The heart of the system lies in its sophisticated embedded control unit, meticulously programmed to monitor and manage various parameters of the reservoir. This intelligent system ensures real-time data acquisition, analysis, and decision-making capabilities.

2. PCB Design:
Developed a custom-printed circuit board (PCB) that serves as the central nervous system of the entire control setup. The PCB is designed to optimize signal processing, minimize power consumption, and provide a reliable platform for seamless communication between different components.

3. Servo Motors:
The incorporation of high-precision servo motors enables precise control over the movement of valves and gates within the reservoir. This level of accuracy ensures efficient water flow regulation, preventing wastage and optimizing resource utilization.

4. DC Motors:
To enhance the versatility of the system, DC motors have been strategically employed to drive specific mechanical components. These motors play a crucial role in automating processes such as pump control, contributing to the overall efficiency of the reservoir management system.

5. RGBs:
RGBs have been integrated into the system to provide visual indicators of the reservoir's status and operational modes. The use of color-coded feedback enhances user understanding and facilitates quick decision-making.
